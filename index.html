Measuring Poverty in NYC

How do policy makers in New York City calculate the city's poverty rate? NYC faces some unique challenges due to its size and cost of living. In the next few minutes, we'll show you how the city calculates whether or not a family is in poverty.

What is the poverty threshold in NYC?

The number of adults and children in a household determine that household's poverty level. If a family's income is less than their poverty threshold, they are in poverty.

How do we know if a family is above their poverty threshold?

The Center for Economic Opportunity calculates whether a household is above or below the poverty threshold using a simple formula. Hover over each part of the formula for more details.
