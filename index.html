Visualizing Poverty in New York City

New York City uses an alternative poverty measure determined by the Center of Economic Opportunity. This poverty threshold reflects the higher cost of housing in New York City and the importance of non-cash benefits. This visualization can be used to understand what factors determine whether or not a family is in poverty. It is important to remember that this visualization is not a calculator and that all figures are estimates.
